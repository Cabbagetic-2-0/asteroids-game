# How to play:

**Desktop:** Use Arrow Keys (or WASD) to move and rotate. Press SPACE to shoot.

**Mobile:** Use the on-screen touch D-Pad and Fire button.


### Objective:
Shoot the square asteroids. They split into smaller squares when hit. Avoid collisions!
